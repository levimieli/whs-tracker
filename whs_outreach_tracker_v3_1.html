⚙ Settings & Outlook Integration
Configure your sender details and connect Microsoft 365
Azure App Setup — Step by Step
Free Microsoft account required · Takes ~10 minutes
How to get your Azure App Client ID
1
Go to portal.azure.com
Sign in with your Microsoft 365 / Outlook account at portal.azure.com. Use the same account you send emails from.
2
Open App Registrations
Search for App registrations in the top search bar and click it. Then click + New registration.
3
Register your app
Name it anything (e.g. "WHS Outreach Tracker"). Under Supported account types choose Accounts in any organizational directory and personal Microsoft accounts. Under Redirect URI choose Single-page application (SPA) and paste in the full path to this HTML file (e.g. file:///C:/Users/Levi/whs_tracker.html). Click Register.
4
Copy your Client ID
On the app overview page, copy the Application (client) ID — it looks like xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx. Paste it into the Settings panel in this tracker.
5
Add Mail.Send permission
In the left menu click API permissions → Add a permission → Microsoft Graph → Delegated permissions. Search for and add Mail.Send and User.Read. Then click Grant admin consent (or ask your IT admin). Personal Outlook accounts do not require admin consent.
6
Connect in Settings
Paste your Client ID into the Settings panel, click Save & Connect Outlook, and sign in when the Microsoft popup appears. Done — emails send directly from your Outlook account and appear in your Sent folder.
✓ MAILTO FALLBACK ALWAYS AVAILABLE
Even without completing Azure setup, the Open in Outlook ↗ button on every email launches your Outlook desktop app with the To, Subject and Body pre-filled. This works immediately with zero setup.
